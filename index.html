Classic Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the eggs and milk, then mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  4. Pour 1/4 cup of the batter into the pan for each pancake.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
